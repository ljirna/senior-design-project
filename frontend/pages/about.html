About ZIM COMMERCE

Our Story

Founded in 1998, amidst a period of rebuilding and renewal, ZiM Commerce began with a simple, heartfelt vision: to bring beautiful, well-crafted furniture within everyone's reach. What started as a single, humble shop on a local street corner was built on the principles of durability, honest value, and a belief that a welcoming home is the foundation of a good life. For years, we served our neighbors, learning the rhythms of our community and the specific needs of Bosnian households. We listened, adapted, and slowly, our reputation for reliable quality and personable service began to grow. As the digital age dawned, we saw an opportunity to extend our commitment beyond our city limits, evolving from a beloved local shop into a pioneering force in furniture e-commerce.

Our growth has been organic, rooted in the trust of our customers. While our catalog has expanded and our logistics network has grown more sophisticated, our core mission remains unchanged: to be your local partner in creating a home you love, wherever you are in our corner of the world.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable furniture that combines style, comfort, and durability, making beautiful homes accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted furniture retailer, known for exceptional quality, customer service, and sustainable practices.
